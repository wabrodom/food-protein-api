Food Protein API

This app will provide good information about protein-rich foods, including the amount of protein content per serving size.

To maintain good health, it is crucial to consume an adequate amount of protein daily.
Healthy protein sources include eggs, nuts, lean meats, fish, dairy products, and select grains.

Endpoint: https://food-protein-api.onrender.com/api/eggs

Example: "quinoa": { "size": "One cup", "grams": 185, "protein": 8 }
